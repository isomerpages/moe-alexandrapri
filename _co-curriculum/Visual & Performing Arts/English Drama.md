<h4><strong>English Drama</strong></h4>
<p>The English Drama Club is a fun and creative space where students explore
the magic of storytelling and performance. Through engaging games, role-play
activities, and short plays, our members build confidence while strengthening
their speaking and listening skills. Our members learn to express emotions
effectively, articulate with clear pronunciation, and collaborate meaningfully
as a team.</p>
<p>We nurture imagination, creativity, and respect for one another’s ideas,
creating a supportive environment where every child feels valued. Our members
actively participate in the Singapore Youth Festival, gaining valuable
performance experience and stage exposure.</p>
<p>In 2025, we proudly participated in the International Science Drama Competition
for the first time and achieved the Merit Award — a significant milestone
for our club.</p>
<p>The English Drama Club is truly a wonderful place for young stars to shine
and discover the joy of acting.</p>
