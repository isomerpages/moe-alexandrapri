<h4><strong>Arts &amp; Crafts</strong></h4>
<p>The Art &amp; Craft Club nurtures students’ creativity and imagination
through hands-on exploration of a wide range of media, including digital
illustration, painting, batik art, watercolour, oil pastels and clay. Students
are encouraged to experiment, express their ideas confidently, and develop
their artistic skills through meaningful art-making experiences.</p>
<p>Selected Art Club members will also have the opportunity to represent
the school in the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Exhibition, showcasing
their artworks on a national platform.</p>
