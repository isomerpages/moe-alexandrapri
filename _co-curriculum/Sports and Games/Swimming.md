<h4><strong>Swimming</strong></h4>
<p>The Swimming CCA develops students' aquatic skills through comprehensive
training in all four competitive strokes. Our programme builds technical
proficiency whilst fostering important values including discipline, teamwork,
and resilience.</p>
<p>Students train at a well-equipped external swimming facility under professional
coaching. The programme caters to all ability levels, from beginners focusing
on water safety and basic techniques to advanced swimmers preparing for
competitive events.</p>
<p>Dedicated swimmers have the opportunity to represent the school at the
National School Games, competing against other schools in various swimming
events. This competitive pathway allows students to showcase their skills
whilst developing school pride and sporting excellence.</p>
<p>Through regular training sessions, students improve their fitness, learn
goal-setting, and develop mental toughness that benefits them both in and
out of the pool. The Swimming CCA provides a supportive environment where
every student can achieve their aquatic potential.</p>
