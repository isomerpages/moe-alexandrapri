<h4><strong>Softball</strong></h4>
<p>The Softball CCA provides students with a platform to develop both their
athletic abilities and personal character through structured training and
competitive play. Students will learn and refine fundamental softball skills
such as throwing and catching, batting techniques, pitching, fielding,
and effective base running. Through regular training sessions, students
are guided to improve their coordination, agility, strength, and game awareness.
<br>
<br>Beyond technical skills, the Softball CCA places strong emphasis on character
development. Students are encouraged to demonstrate sportsmanship, discipline,
resilience, and teamwork as they train and compete together. They learn
to support one another, handle challenges positively, and persevere through
both victories and setbacks.</p>
<p>Selected members will represent the school in the Softball National School
Games (NSG) and other inter-school competitions, providing valuable exposure
to competitive environments. Through these experiences, students gain confidence,
leadership skills, and a strong sense of school pride while striving for
excellence both on and off the field.</p>
