<h4><strong>InfoComm Technology (Computing)</strong></h4>
<p>InfoComm Technology (Computing) CCA is the perfect place to nurture your
budding interest in emerging technology! We offer a wide range of fun and
engaging activities, from mobile app development and game development to
coding with AI. We believe in the potential of each student, and our activities
are designed to challenge students' creativity, resilience, and logical
thinking. The InfoComm Technology (Computing) CCA promises opportunities
for hands-on learning experiences. Join us today and explore the world
of information and communication technology!</p>
