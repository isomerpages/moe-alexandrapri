<h4><strong>InfoComm Technology (Computing)</strong></h4>
<p>InfoComm Technology (Computing) CCA is the perfect platform to nurture
your budding interest in emerging technologies and digital innovation!
<br>
<br>We offer a wide range of exciting and hands-on experiences, including:
<br>● LEGO Robotics
<br>● Game Development
<br>● Digital Making &amp; Prototyping
<br>● Coding with AI
<br>
<br>Through these engaging activities, students develop:
<br>● Creative problem-solving skills
<br>● Logical and computational thinking
<br>● Resilience and perseverance
<br>● Confidence in innovation and design</p>
<p>Our CCA provides opportunities for students to experiment, collaborate,
and bring their ideas to life in a supportive and dynamic environment.</p>
<p>Join us and discover the endless possibilities in the world of Information
and Communication Technology!</p>
