<h4><strong>Cheerleading</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<p>The Cheerleading CCA empowers students to develop resilience, teamwork,
and discipline through training in stunts and gymnastics, performed to
upbeat, high-energy music. Cheerleaders will have opportunities to represent
the school with pride at both school and public events. Through cheerleading,
students not only enhance their physical fitness and coordination, but
also grow in leadership and confidence, embodying the spirit of excellence
and collaboration.</p>
<p>ALPS Cheerleading CCA Featured in Thumbs Up (《大拇指》)
<br>We are proud to share that <a href="http://zbschools.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">zbschools.sg</a> featured our ALPS Cheerleading
CCA in the 2025 Issue No. 6 of Thumbs Up (《大拇指》), a Chinese publication
for students. The article, titled “我的CCA很特别” (My CCA is Special), highlights
the passion and teamwork of our cheerleaders.</p>
