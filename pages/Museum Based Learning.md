<h4><strong>P3 Museum-based Learning @ Geylang Serai Heritage Gallery</strong></h4>
<p>Primary 3 students participated in a learning journey to explore the distinctive
architecture, trades and heritage of Geylang Serai. At four interactive
stations, students engaged in activities that introduced the history and
development of the area, as well as the rich Malay-Muslim culture and traditions
associated with it. Through guided fieldwork, students learned about the
contributions of the Malay and Muslim communities and listened to stories
about growing up and living in Geylang Serai. The experience helped students
deepen their appreciation of Singapore’s multicultural heritage and its
importance in shaping our shared identity.</p>
