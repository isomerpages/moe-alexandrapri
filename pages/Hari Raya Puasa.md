<h4><strong>Hari Raya Puasa</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To celebrate Hari Raya Aidilfitri through a series of engaging activities
that provide meaningful opportunities for students to learn about its traditions
and customs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To deepen students’ understanding of Malay heritage and the cultural practices
associated with Hari Raya Aidilfitri.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote the values of Harmony, Empathy, Respect, Optimism, and Creativity
within the ALPS community.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Programme Highlights</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Pre-celebration activities in classroom during <br>CCE MTL/EL lessons</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Pop-up Hari Raya card</strong>
<br>- To express appreciation, share well wishes, and spread joy while strengthening
relationships with others.</p>
