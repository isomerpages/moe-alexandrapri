<p><strong>International Friendship Day 2026</strong>
</p>
<p>To commemorate this special day, our students participated in an assembly
programme where they learned about the importance of fostering friendships
between countries through a presentation led by a group of Primary 4 students.
This was followed by captivating performances showcasing how different
types of flutes reflect the cultural heritage and musical traditions of
their respective countries of origin.</p>
<p>Adding to the festive spirit, many students came dressed in beautiful
traditional costumes from around the world. Throughout the week, students
also explored exhibitions curated for each level, featuring Indonesia,
Thailand, The Philippines, India, Japan and Vietnam. These exhibitions
were designed in a World Café format to allow students to learn about different
aspects like art, music and books from each culture in an engaging and
interactive way.</p>
